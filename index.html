The heart of it

Your Students Are Ready to Go Deeper — They Just Need the Right Moment

You see it every week. Students who love Jesus but whose faith still feels distant — something they believe but haven't fully experienced. They're ready for more. They just haven't had the moment that makes it real.

That moment happens when they step outside their comfort zone, pick up a paintbrush for a stranger, and see God show up in ways no classroom or sermon can replicate.

Meanwhile, thousands of low-income families across America need exactly the kind of help your students can give. Porches need rebuilding. Ramps need building. And in serving those families, students encounter Jesus in a way that marks them forever.

The summers that shape a student's faith aren't the ones they forget by August. They're the ones where they got on their knees — to paint a porch and to pray with a homeowner. That's what World Changers creates.

The life change

They Leave as Students. They Come Home as Disciples.

Construction is what we do. Life change is what happens. Here's what a week of serving others produces in the hearts of your students.

🔨

They discover what they were made for

Something clicks when a student builds a wheelchair ramp for a grandmother who's been stuck inside for months. They realize God made them to serve — and that following Jesus means getting their hands dirty.

✨

Their faith becomes their own

Nightly worship. Morning crew devos. Gospel conversations with homeowners. For many students, this is the week faith stops being their parents' and becomes theirs — real, personal, and rooted in Jesus.

🏠

They see God move — through them

When a homeowner tears up and thanks your student by name, something eternal happens. Students see firsthand that God uses ordinary people to do extraordinary things — and they never forget it.
